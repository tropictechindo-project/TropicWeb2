7.  **Intent-Based Prompting & Prefetching**: Location/Notification permissions and page data prefetching are intelligently delayed or triggered based on user intent (hover/click) to maximize loading speed.
8.  **Worker Dispatch Automation**: Automatic assignment of unclaimed `QUEUED` jobs to available workers after 60 minutes.
9.  **Real-time Data Sync**: Instant bidirectional synchronization for communications (Messages/Notifications) via Supabase WebSockets.
10. **"Instant Page" Automation**: Automated LCP prioritization and dynamic `next/dynamic` chunking with Skeleton fallbacks.
11. **Deep Site Settings Sync**: Automated JSON parsing and synchronization between Admin Dashboard settings and Landing Page views (Gallery/Configs).
